Transform & Roll Out!

ABOUT

This series documents real-play sessions of roleplaying campaigns that use our Homebrewed Transformers D&D ruleset.

Each season a brand new story and continuity, such as original voice actors like Frank Todaro (Starcream), Jason Marnocha (Megatron), and many more.

SEASON 4

Reforged

Reforge is a story of sparklings navigating their way through Cybertron, but not everything is as it seems. Follow our characters: protoforms Aqua, Bloodstorm, Spyder-Byte, and Radar as they discover who they are and what they want to do while Cybrtron plunges into chaos around them.

SEASON 3

Eons

Beginning with a one-off episode starring Elita-1's original voice actor Linsay Rousseau reprising the role, this season continues the story for characters Scorchfire Prime, Mantyx, Grease, and Smolder. This continuity clashes with season 2 characters and more. Our heroes spiral through different timelines, meet variations of characters, and must fight against the evil forces of Galaxywave and Unicron. Oh and Starscream is here too. Will they survive?

SEASON 2

First Stand of The Wreckers

Follow characters Allegory, Frost Fang, Synthwave, Cross Summit, and Borg who find themselves in the wrong place at the wrong time. They must now solve a mystery to clear their names. A new DM, a new story, new characters.
